University Information Systems Risk Assessment

Comprehensive security evaluation using OCTAVE Allegro methodology

Project Overview

Applied the OCTAVE Allegro risk assessment methodology to evaluate security risks in a university's academic and information systems. Conducted stakeholder interviews, asset prioritization, and impact mapping to identify key threats and propose mitigation strategies.

My Role

As team leader, I facilitated asset evaluation, CIA impact mapping, and risk scenario identification based on threat sources and vulnerabilities. Structured the risk analysis worksheet and coordinated team presentations to stakeholders.

Outcomes

Delivered a comprehensive risk assessment report with prioritized mitigation strategies. Improved the university's security posture by identifying critical vulnerabilities in their information systems and academic processes.

OCTAVE Allegro Methodology

1

Establish Drivers

Identified organizational objectives, compliance requirements, and risk tolerance levels through stakeholder interviews.

2

Profile Assets

Cataloged and prioritized critical assets including student data systems, research databases, and administrative platforms.

3

Identify Threats

Mapped potential threats to each asset considering both internal and external threat actors.

4

Mitigate Risks

Developed prioritized risk mitigation strategies with cost-benefit analysis for implementation.
