Project Objective

Conducted comprehensive profiling of five active hacker groups in Indonesia (2024-2025) using advanced OSINT techniques including social media investigation, framework-based analysis, and digital footprint mapping.

Investigation Methodology

Combined OSINT tools with structured frameworks (MITRE ATT&CK, OSINT Framework) and social media analysis to uncover identities, infrastructure, and operational patterns while maintaining ethical boundaries.

Identified operational links between groups, mapped infrastructure, and developed threat actor profiles with TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures) for each group.
