Stay Healthy App

Your all-in-one health and fitness companion

Project Objective

To create a comprehensive health and fitness application that integrates multiple wellness tools into a single platform, addressing the fragmentation of current health solutions and providing users with a holistic approach to maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Key Features

Step tracking, exercise videos, yoga tutorials, diet planning, community messaging, and 24/7 email support - all integrated into one user-friendly application with intuitive navigation and visual guidance.

Unique Value

Unlike single-purpose fitness apps, Stay Healthy combines all essential health tools with community support features, making it easier for users to stay motivated and consistent in their wellness journey.

The Problem

Current health solutions are fragmented across multiple apps, lacking integration and community support. Users struggle with:

  • Switching between different apps for different needs
  • Lack of motivation due to isolation
  • Overwhelming and confusing guidance
  • Insufficient customer support

Our Solution

Stay Healthy integrates all essential health tools into one platform:

  • Comprehensive fitness tracking and guidance
  • Built-in community for motivation
  • Personalized diet plans
  • 24/7 support system
  • User-friendly interface with visual cues

Implementation Process

1

User Research

Identified key pain points in existing health apps through surveys and interviews, determining the need for an integrated solution with community support.

2

Feature Design

Designed core features including step tracking, exercise videos, yoga tutorials, diet plans, and community messaging with intuitive UI/UX.

3

Database Design

Created ERD and data dictionary for user accounts, step data, messages, videos, and diet plans with appropriate relationships.

4

UI Development

Implemented all screens including signup/login, home page, step tracker, exercise/yoga videos, diet plans, and messenger with responsive design.

5

Testing & Refinement

Conducted user testing to validate functionality and usability, refining features based on feedback before final implementation.
